What is LCM?

The LCM (Least Common Multiple) of two numbers is the smallest number that can be divided by both numbers.

Example: The LCM of 4 and 6 is 12 because 12 is the smallest number that can be divided by both 4 and 6.

Tip: You can find it by listing multiples or using the GCD formula!
